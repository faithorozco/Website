Our Story

Three piece blues/grunge outfit from Fresno, CA. With front
man/guitar player Dylan Russell's hard rock and melodic influences and bass player
Faith Orozco's punk/funk influences all propelled by Darian Ramirez's jazzy
percussive drumming, Artesia fills a room with a melodic cacophony. Artesia reigns from
Clovis, but originated in Faith's garage sophomore year of high school in
Reedley, California. Artesia realized they had something special after they
wrote and rehearsed original material such as "Princess" and "Lost My Mind".
They kept it up to write a full length Lo-Fi debut "Walkalina" and continue to
gig in the Central CA area to create an audience. They will play
anytime/anywhere: a garage, a backyard, a party, a wedding, a funeral they just wanna blow
up your ears with their sweet music.
